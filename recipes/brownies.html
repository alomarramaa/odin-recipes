Brownies

Description

This recipe creates delicious, fudgey brownies that can be easily made at home!

Ingredients

  • 10 tablespoons (145 grams) unsalted butter
  • 1 Â¼ cups (250 grams) granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons (80 grams) unsweetened cocoa powder, natural or Dutch-process
  • 1/4 rounded teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large cold eggs
  • 1/2 cup (65 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup (75 grams) chopped walnuts or pecans

Steps

  1. Melt the butter with sugar, salt, and cocoa powder
  2. Add the vanilla and eggs
  3. Add the flour
  4. Bake the brownies at 325 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes
